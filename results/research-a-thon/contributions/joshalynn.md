# Team Member Contribution: JoshaLynn Worth

## ⚖️ Project Role: Results Evaluation & Ethics

For this project, JoshaLynn led the quantitative validation of the model's performance and established the project's ethical guidelines for the deployment of Affective Computing technologies.

### 📊 Key Technical Contributions:
*   **Quantitative Performance Evaluation:** Developed the metrics pipeline (Accuracy, Macro F1, Precision, Recall) across all 8 emotion and 2 intensity classes.
*   **Arousal Variance Study:** Analyzed and documented the model's significant performance differences between high-arousal (Angry/Happy) and low-arousal (Calm/Neutral) vocal tones.
*   **Per-Actor Performance Discovery:** Conducted the per-actor variance analysis, identifying the impact of speaker-specific emotional expression styles on model consistency.
*   **Ethical Impact Framework:** Authored the comprehensive study on Algorithmic Bias, demonstrating the risks of demographic skew in Emotion AI datasets.
*   **Privacy Protocols:** Designed the guidelines for "Local Inference" and "Data Ephemerality" to protect user voice recordings.

### 📈 Strategic Impact:
JoshaLynn’s rigorous validation and focus on algorithmic fairness transformed this from a research project into an ethically responsible and scientifically validated AI pipeline.
