# Team Member Contribution: Diego Brown

## Project Role: Audio Preprocessing & Data Augmentation Lead

As the preprocessing lead, Diego focused on the critical stage of transforming raw sound into mathematical data for the neural network.

### Key Contributions:
*   **Audio Parsing:** Implemented the raw `.wav` loading logic and standardized sampling rates across the RAVDESS dataset.
*   **Feature Extraction:** Developed the scripts for extracting MFCCs (Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients) and Mel Spectrograms using the Librosa library.
*   **Data Augmentation:** Engineered the noise-injection, pitch-shifting, and time-stretching logic crucial for the model's robustness.
*   **Data Cleaning:** Handled silence trimming and volume normalization to ensure consistent input amplitude.
*   **Preprocessing Pipeline:** Constructed the automated script that prepares the training and testing arrays for the architecture lead.
